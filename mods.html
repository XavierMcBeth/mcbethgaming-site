Current Server Mods

Server-side friendly quality-of-life enhancements for the McBeth Gaming community server.

The McBeth Gaming mod collection is designed to improve the 7 Days to Die experience with better quality-of-life features, smoother progression, expanded storage, enhanced building systems, improved loot, and more fun community gameplay.

Most of these mods are server-side and client-friendly, meaning players can join and play without needing to download anything extra.

Large overhaul mods, such as Darkness Falls, require players to install the mod pack before joining due to the massive gameplay, progression, and world changes they introduce.
