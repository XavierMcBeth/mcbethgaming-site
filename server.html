How to Join

The server is community-focused, not a wide-open public server. To be considered for access, you must follow McBeth Gaming or BoogieFaye00 and stay active in the community.

This helps us protect the server from trolls, griefing, and random players who are not connected to the community.

  • Follow McBeth Gaming or BoogieFaye00.
  • Be active in the community through streams, chat, TikTok, Twitch, or server updates.
  • Ask about access during stream or through the current community contact method.
  • Wait for approval. Server login details will not be posted publicly.
  • Once approved, join in, keep it family friendly, and survive with the crew.

Every survivor starts with access to the community server and can earn additional perks by supporting the community.

Our goal is to create a fun, family-friendly community where survivors can make friends, conquer horde nights, and enjoy the apocalypse together.

Server Rules

The goal is simple: keep the server fun, fair, and welcoming for everyone who earns access to the community world.

  • Keep it family friendly.
  • No griefing, stealing, or ruining other players’ builds.
  • Respect other survivors and community members.
  • Follow server announcements, mod rules, and event rules.
  • Help keep the McBeth Gaming community fun and welcoming.
